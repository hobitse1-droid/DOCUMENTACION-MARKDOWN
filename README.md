### Determinantes 2x2

\begin{pmatrix}

3 & 1 & \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$ B =
\begin{pmatrix}
-1 & 4 & \\

\end{pmatrix}
$$

Det(B)= 8 - 8 = 0

$$ C =
\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix}
$$

Det(C)= 18 - 18 = 0

$$ D =
\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix}

Det(D)= 0 + 25 = 25
